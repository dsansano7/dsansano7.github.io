I am a Game Audio Developer and Sound Designer based in Valencia, Spain. I hold a degree in Game Design and Development (UJI) and now, I'm taking a Master’s in Game Audio from Berklee College of Music.

My technical expertise is rooted in a strong musical foundation as a Professional Conservatory Graduate in Saxophone. This background allows me to approach game audio with a "musician’s ear", bringing a unique sensitivity to rhythm, timbre, and emotional pacing to interactive worlds.
