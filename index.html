📝 Biography

I'm a Game Audio Developer passionate about shaping the sonic identity of interactive experiences. My work sits at the intersection of technical audio programming and creative sound design — building the systems that make games feel alive.

I hold a University Degree in Video Game Design and Development from Universitat Jaume I (UJI), am currently completing a Master's in Game Audio at Berklee College of Music, and hold a Professional Music Degree in Saxophone. That classical foundation gives me a musician's ear that elevates everything from foley to adaptive scores.
